Our Methodology

The Transformation Process

From massive quarry boulders to flawless architectural surfaces. See how Jamna Stone engineers raw earth into premium assets.

01
⛰️

Quarry Extraction

We source massive, high-density granite blocks directly from elite quarries, ensuring consistent color and structural integrity.

02
⚙️

Multi-Wire Sawing

Blocks are sliced into slabs using state-of-the-art Italian multi-wire saws, ensuring uniform thickness and minimal wastage.

03
✨

Resin & Polishing

Automated epoxy lines fill microscopic fissures, followed by 21-head line polishers that achieve a flawless, mirror-like finish.

04
🔍

Quality Assurance

Every single slab undergoes rigorous laser inspection for hairline cracks, polish consistency, and dimension accuracy before crating.

Corporate Infrastructure

Industrial Scale Fabrication

Advanced CNC Processing

We utilize state-of-the-art multi-axis CNC bridge saws and waterjet technology to achieve exacting tolerances for complex architectural elements. Standard yards cut with high margins of error; our laser-guided systems ensure your slabs and tiles align perfectly.

Custom Profiles: Precise edge detailing including ogee, bullnose, and complex waterfall joints.
High-Yield Cutting: AI-assisted digital templating minimizes material waste, saving costs.

Global Export Logistics

Jamna Stone is an established exporter, reliably shipping high-tonnage containers of raw blocks and finished slabs to markets worldwide directly from our fabrication facilities.

Secure Crating: Fumigated, heavy-duty ISPM-15 compliant wooden bundles ensure zero breakage.
Streamlined Customs: Dedicated export documentation team handling Bill of Lading and port clearances.
