The Transformation Process

From massive quarry boulders to flawless architectural surfaces. See how Jamna Stone engineers raw earth into premium granite.

01
⛏️

Quarry Extraction

We source massive, high-density granite blocks directly from elite quarries, ensuring consistent color and structural integrity.

02
⚙️

Multi-Wire Sawing

Blocks are sliced into slabs using state-of-the-art Italian multi-wire saws, ensuring uniform thickness and minimal wastage.

03
✨

Resin & Polishing

Automated epoxy lines fill microscopic fissures, followed by 21-head line polishers that achieve a flawless, mirror-like finish.

04
🔍

Quality Assurance

Every single slab undergoes rigorous laser inspection for hairline cracks, polish consistency, and dimension accuracy before crating.
